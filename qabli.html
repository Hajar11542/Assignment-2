Qabli


Ingredients

  • Rice
  • meat
  • carrots
  • raisins
  • oil


A step-by-step instruction to prepare this food

  1. Cook the meat.
  2. Cook rice with it.
  3. Layer sweet carrots and raisons.
  4. Layer the meat at the bottom.
  5. steam it.
  6. fluff the rice and serve it to your beloved family and enjoy eating.
