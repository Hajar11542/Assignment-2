Ashak

Ingredients

  • dough
  • beans
  • garlic chives
  • oil
  • onion
  • pepper
  • garlic


A step-by-step instruction to prepare this food

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Chop the garlic chives and mix it with alittle salt.
  3. Roll out the dough.
  4. Place the garlic chives in the center of each wrapper and fold.
  5. Boil them and prepare the beans sauce.
  6. prepare a yogurt sauce.
  7. Now, it's time to plate and serve to your beloved family.
